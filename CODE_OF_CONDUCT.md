# Code Of Conduct

Artisan contributors are expected to keep project spaces focused, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still asking for clear technical evidence.
- Keep criticism focused on code, behavior, or project impact.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about product scope and performance constraints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive comments about a person's identity or background.
- Repeated off-topic arguments after maintainers have redirected the discussion.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or PRs; or block contributors when behavior harms the project.
